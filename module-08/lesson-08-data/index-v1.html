Proportional Symbol Sizes of Alpine Area Cities

Map authored by John Davis

This map shows the proportional symbol size of 4 cities around the Alps region of Europe. Each city has a circle marker representing the area of the city. The smallest sized city is Lucerne, Switzerland, while Innsbruck, Austria is the next largest. North of the Alps Mountain range is Munich, Germany. Finally, the largest city in area is the beautiful Venice, Italy.
