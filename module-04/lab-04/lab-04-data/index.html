3 Major Cities I've Visited

A Sojourn Across the Globe

Map authored by John Davis

Since the turn of the century, I have had several fortunate opportunities to travel abroad to many different countries and some of their most populous cities. Traveling to these places has allowed me to accomplish one of my life goals of visiting all 7 continents, although Antarctica might prove difficult to reach.

In 2003, I went on a trip to Australia visiting Sydney and all its marvelous cultural and educational institutions. We visited the Sydney Opera House, strolled through the Royal Botanic Gardens, and conquered the famous Sydney Harbor Bridge Climb! I can't wait to go back.

My next international trip was in Europe visiting 7 countries in 2 weeks with Paris as one of our main destinations. While there, we took a ride down the Champs Elysees, saw the Notre Dame Cathedral, and enjoyed a River Cruise down the Seine toward the Eiffel Tower gleaming in the night sky.

My most recent trip was to China and Hong Kong, a city of bustling marketplaces and picturesque city views. We packed in so much in only 2 days including a ride on the Peak Tram, seeing the world's second largest Buddha, and enjoyed the intriguing cuisine and exciting nightlife offered in one of Asia's most eclectic cities.

I can't wait to travel the world next year to some truly relaxing and exotic locales!
