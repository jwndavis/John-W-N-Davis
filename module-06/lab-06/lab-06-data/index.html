3 Cities of Different Hemispherical Latitudes

Map authored by John Davis

Close to the equator, Costa Rica is a wonderous and adventurous place to visit. As the capital of the country, San Jose is where you can visit educational museums, restaurants, markets, and spend a night on the town.

Settled north of the Tropic of Cancer at 470 degrees north in the Alps, Lucerne, Switzerland is the home of Bucherer watches and jewelery, chocolate, and Mount Pilatus. With stunning mountain vistas, the Swiss Alps region is one of my favorites!

Lastly, nestled well south of the Tropic of Capricorn is the town of Rotorua, New Zealand. This city is known for its famous hot springs. Not overshadowed by these natural geothermal attractions, you can also bungy jump and enjoy a nice sheep shearing demonstration. There are plenty of exciting countries and cities to explore all over the world. Go get your passport today!
